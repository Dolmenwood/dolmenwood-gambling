House Rules

The Flow

1. Cheating (Optional): Attempt to influence the dice. High risk, high reward.

2. Gambling: The main roll. Determines if you win or lose your stake.

3. Complication: If you win, you might attract unwanted attention from the Thieves' Guild or jealous rivals.

Cheating Table (2d6 + Mod)

  • 2 or less: Caught! Heavy penalty.
  • 3–5: Suspected. Small penalty.
  • 6–8: Unnoticed. No bonus.
  • 9–11: Effective. +2 to Gambling.
  • 12+: Very Effective. +4 to Gambling.

Gambling Table (2d6 + Bonus)

  • 2–5: Loss.
  • 6–8: Break even.
  • 9–11: 2× Win (Triggers Complication).
  • 12+: 10× Win (Triggers Complication).

Complication Table (1d6 + Mods)

  • 1 or less: The Guild takes notice (Bad).
  • 2–4: Hostility and Reputation damage.
  • 5+: Safe... for tonight.
